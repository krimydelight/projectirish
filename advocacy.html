VALUING GOOD MENTAL HEALTH FOR BETTER EDUCATION

As a student, it is normal to feel stressed and exhausted especially when deadlines are approaching. Maintaining high grades is not that easy especially now that students are still adjusting with the new normal. Good mental health is very important to improve academic performance of the students and to actively maintain their appetite in studying. Education should not be a gateway for depression and any mental illness, it should be both the knowledge and solution in coping up this kind of phenomena. It should be learning and creating comfortable space for the students.
